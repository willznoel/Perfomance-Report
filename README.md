# 🌱 Plant Co. Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the sales performance of Plant Co. across different countries, product types, and time periods.  
The goal of the dashboard is to help stakeholders monitor revenue trends, profitability, and product performance while comparing current year performance with the previous year.

The dashboard allows users to explore key business metrics such as Year-to-Date (YTD) sales, Gross Profit percentage, and sales growth across regions and product categories.

---

# 📊 Key Business Questions
This analysis answers several important business questions:

- How are sales performing compared to the previous year?
- Which countries have the lowest sales performance?
- Which product categories contribute most to revenue?
- How profitable are different accounts?
- What are the monthly sales trends?

---

# 📈 Key Metrics
The dashboard tracks several performance indicators including:

- **YTD Sales**
- **Previous Year Sales (PYTD)**
- **Sales Growth (YTD vs PYTD)**
- **Gross Profit Percentage (GP%)**
- **Product Sales Distribution**

---

# 📉 Dashboard Features

## Sales Performance Dashboard
This dashboard provides an overview of:

- Sales performance across months
- Product category contribution (Indoor, Landscape, Outdoor)
- Sales growth compared to the previous year
- Bottom-performing countries by sales

## Quantity Performance Dashboard
This dashboard focuses on product quantities sold across different regions and time periods, helping identify trends in product demand.

## Profitability Segmentation
A scatter plot visualizes account profitability using:

- **Gross Profit Percentage**
- **Sales Value**

This helps identify high-value and high-profit customer segments.
